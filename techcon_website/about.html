Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local event for developers and innovators. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the world’s largest technology conferences, attracting thousands of participants from over 50 countries each year.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring together thinkers, innovators, and creators to share ideas that shape the future of technology. TechCon fosters collaboration, inspires innovation, and promotes diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Smith

Dr. Jane Smith is a pioneer in artificial intelligence ethics and has spoken about responsible AI practices at TechCon since 2018. Her research has influenced AI policy globally.

Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson, CEO of CloudNet, shared insights on the future of cloud computing and how companies can adapt to distributed infrastructure.

Priya Desai

Priya Desai, a cybersecurity expert, discussed modern strategies to protect data and networks in an increasingly connected world.
